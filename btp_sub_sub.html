Includes:

77 question cards

Objective of the Game:

To be the first player OR team to earn five cards.

Instructions

1) Separate into Parents and Kids teams.

2)Shuffle the cards and place them face down. The Kids team answer the first question.

3)The Parents team picks a player to be the reader and draws a card from the top of the deck. The reader asks the Kids team to choose a question number between 1 and 3.

4)Once the Kids team makes their choice, the Parents reader reads the chosen question from the Ask the Kids section on the card.

5)If the Kids team answers the question correctly, they keep the card and play moves to the other team.

6) If incorrectly answered, their turn ends, and the card is placed to the bottom of the deck, and it is now the other teamâ€™s turn.

Winner: The first team to earn five cards!
