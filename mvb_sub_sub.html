Millennials VS Boomers - Rules!

Includes:

1,000 questions that cover five categories: Geography, History, Pop culture, Sports, and General trivia.

75 Photo cards

Objective of the Game:

To answer correctly and obtain them most cards.

Instructions

1) Gather 2 or more friends.

2)The box has an in-built separator that has the Millennial and Boomer question cards. Team members will sit in correspondence to their age group.

3)Each player/team will take turns asking the other team a question. The first player/team can be determined by a coin toss (coin not included).

4)If answered correctly, the card goes to the player/team that answered correctly.

5)The player/team that has the most cards at the end wins

Winner: The player who gets the most cards at the end of the game!
